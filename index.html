About George

Built From
Experience.

I've been training since the age of 16. I've made every mistake in the book — and more importantly, I've learned exactly how to avoid them. That hard-won knowledge is what I put into every programme I write.

For nine years I worked in engineering whilst training and coaching on the side. I know what it's like to be genuinely busy — to have a full diary, a demanding job, and still want to show up for your health. That's why I build programmes around your life, not an idealised version of it.

My belief is simple: you should feel comfortable in how you look and feel. Whatever your starting point, I'll build something you can stick to — and actually enjoy.
